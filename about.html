About TechKnowGreen

Empowering Kenya with sustainable water and energy solutions since 2020.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, TechKnowGreen is a leading provider of sustainable water and energy solutions in Kenya. Based in Mombasa, we specialize in solar power systems, borehole services, water pumps, water treatment, and energy-efficient technologies. Our mission is to empower communities and businesses with eco-friendly solutions that reduce environmental impact and promote long-term sustainability.

Our journey began with a vision to address the pressing need for reliable and sustainable water and energy solutions in Kenya. Over the years, we have grown from a small startup to a trusted name in the industry, completing over 500 projects across the country. From residential solar installations to large-scale borehole systems, our team brings a wealth of expertise and a commitment to excellence to every project.

At TechKnowGreen, we believe in harnessing the power of innovation to create a greener future. Our solutions are designed to meet the unique needs of our clients, whether they are rural households seeking clean water or businesses aiming to reduce their carbon footprint. We pride ourselves on our ability to deliver high-quality, customized solutions that combine cutting-edge technology with environmental stewardship.

Our success is built on a foundation of collaboration, integrity, and a relentless pursuit of quality. With a team of highly skilled engineers, technicians, and sustainability experts, we ensure that every project meets the highest industry standards. Our partnerships with leading global manufacturers allow us to offer state-of-the-art equipment, ensuring reliability and efficiency for our clients.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At TechKnowGreen, sustainability is at the heart of everything we do. We are dedicated to reducing carbon footprints and promoting renewable energy sources across Kenya. Our solar power systems and water management solutions are designed to minimize environmental impact while maximizing efficiency.

We actively work to combat climate change by integrating renewable energy into our projects. From solar-powered water pumps to energy-efficient generators, our solutions help reduce reliance on fossil fuels. Additionally, our water treatment systems ensure that communities have access to clean, safe water without harming the environment.

Our commitment extends beyond our projects. We partner with local and international organizations to promote sustainable practices and educate communities about the benefits of renewable energy. By investing in research and development, we continuously improve our offerings to align with global sustainability goals.

Community Engagement

TechKnowGreen is more than a business; we are a partner in community development. Since our inception, we have worked closely with communities across Kenya to provide access to clean water and reliable energy. Our projects have empowered schools, healthcare facilities, and rural households by delivering sustainable solutions that improve quality of life.

We believe in giving back to the communities we serve. Through our community outreach programs, we offer training on sustainable practices, provide job opportunities, and support local initiatives. Our borehole projects, for example, have brought clean water to over 50 rural communities, reducing waterborne diseases and improving living standards.

We also collaborate with local schools to promote STEM education, inspiring the next generation of innovators in renewable energy and water management. By fostering partnerships with community leaders, we ensure that our projects are tailored to meet local needs and create lasting impact.
