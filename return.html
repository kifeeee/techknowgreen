Our Return Policy

Overview

At TechKnowGreen, we strive to ensure you are completely satisfied with your purchase. If you need to return a product, our return policy outlines the eligibility criteria and steps to make the process as smooth as possible. This policy applies to products purchased directly from our online shop or physical store.

Eligibility for Returns

Products are eligible for return under the following conditions:

  • Return requests must be made within 14 days of delivery or purchase date.
  • Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and in the same condition as received.
  • Proof of purchase (e.g., receipt or order confirmation) is required.
  • Custom-ordered or installed products (e.g., borehole systems, solar installations) are non-returnable unless defective.
  • Defective or damaged items are eligible for return or replacement, subject to inspection.

Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  • Contact our customer service team at info@techknowgreen.co.ke or call (+254)711312029 to request a return.
  • Provide your order number, item details, and reason for return.
  • Our team will provide a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and return instructions.
  • Securely package the item with all original accessories and include the RMA number.
  • Ship the item to our designated return address (provided with RMA). Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or incorrect.

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund or replacement. Approved refunds will be processed within 7 business days to the original payment method.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return:

  • Products used or damaged by the customer.
  • Items without original packaging or missing components.
  • Customized or installed systems unless found defective upon delivery.
  • Products purchased from third-party retailers (please contact the retailer directly).
